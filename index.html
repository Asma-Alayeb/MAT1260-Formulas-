MAT1260 Formula Sheet

Descriptive Statistics

  • Relative frequency = \(\frac{frequency}{\text{sum of all frequencies}}\)
  • Class midpoint = \(\frac{\text{lower class limit + Upper class limit}}{2}\)
  • Mean: \(\mu = \frac{\sum x_i}{n}\)
  • Excel Mean: =AVERAGE(numbers)
  • Population SD: \(\sigma = \sqrt{\frac{\sum (x_i - \mu)^2}{N}}\)
  • Excel population SD: =STDEV.P(numbers)
  • Sample SD: \(s = \sqrt{\frac{\sum (x_i - \bar{x})^2}{n-1}}\)
  • Excel sample SD: =STDEV.S(numbers)

Z-scores and Quartiles

  • Z-score: \(z = \frac{x - \mu}{\sigma}\)
  • Excel z-score: =STANDARDIZE(X, mean, SD)
  • Quartiles: =QUARTILE.INC(numbers,1/2/3)

Correlation and Regression

  • Correlation coefficient \(r\)= =CORREL(array1, array2)
  • Regression line: \(y = mx + b\)
  • Slope: \(m\) =SLOPE(ys,xs)
  • Intercept: \(b\)=INTERCEPT(ys,xs)

Probability Rules

  • General Addition: \(P(A \text{ or } B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A \text{ and } B)\)
  • Complement: \(P(A^c) = 1 - P(A)\)
  • Multiplication: \(P(A \text{ and } B) = P(A) \cdot P(B|A)\)
  • Conditional: \(P(A|B) = \frac{P(A \text{ and } B)}{P(B)}\)

Binomial Distribution

  • Mean: \(\mu_{bi} = n \cdot p\)
  • SD: \(\sigma_{bi} = \sqrt{n \cdot p \cdot (1-p)}\)
  • Excel: =BINOM.DIST(x, trials, probability, FALSE)

Normal Distribution

  • \text{Area to the left of x} \(p(\le)\= : =NORM.DIST(x, Mean, SD, TRUE)
  • Area to the right of x: =1 - NORM.DIST(x, Mean, SD, TRUE)
  • area between a and b: =NORM.DIST(b,Mean,SD,TRUE)-NORM.DIST(a,Mean,SD,TRUE)
  • Inverse: =NORM.INV(area, Mean, SD)

Sampling Distributions

  • Sample mean:
    \(\mu_{\bar{x}} = \mu\)
  • Standard deviation of sample mean:
    \(\sigma_{\bar{x}} = \frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{n}}\)
  • Sample proportion:
    \(\hat{p} = \frac{x}{n}\)
  • Mean of sample proportion:
    \(\mu_{\hat{p}} = p\)
  • Standard deviation of sample proportion:
    \(\sigma_{\hat{p}} = \sqrt{\frac{p(1-p)}{n}}\)
