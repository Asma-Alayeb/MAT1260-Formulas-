Normal Distribution

  • Area to the left: =NORM.DIST(x, Mean, Standard Deviation, TRUE)
  • Area to the right: =1 - NORM.DIST(x, Mean, Standard Deviation, TRUE)
  • Given percentile, find number: =NORM.INV(area, Mean, Standard Deviation)
